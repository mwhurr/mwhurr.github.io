Business Formation & General Business Consulting

In Seattle's vibrant economy sound legal advice is essential. At MSO Law we partner with you throughout the life of your company. From business startup through maturity, our attorneys advise Northwest entrepreneurs in all aspects of business law.

At company formation or acquisition we help you establish a corporation, limited liability company (LLC), or partnership. MSO Law serves as the registered agent for many of its clients. We also draft and review commercial leases, buy-sell agreements, noncompete clauses and employment contracts.

As your business grows we assist you with general business consulting including contracts and contract disputes, employee relations, business litigation and the successful sale or merger of your company.
